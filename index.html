I visit the projects in 3D in this British Museum expedition. All the 3D models are displayed on the Sketchfab and it's open for us to download these 3D models.

Among these collections, I like the Sekhmet the most. It is an 18th Dynasty granodiorite statue of Sekhmet, standing, with papyrus-staff before, base and arms missing or damaged. This sculpture is very symbolic in Egypt culture.

Under the Nikon D5100, 337 photographs are taken by Daniel Pett to liven up this 3D model.
